Name:Bitcoin

Description: Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that operates without a central authority or government. It uses blockchain technology to enable peer-to-peer transactions over the internet, allowing users to send and receive payments securely and transparently. Bitcoin is often referred to as a cryptocurrency, and its limited supply and decentralized nature make it a popular store of value and a hedge against inflation.
